# Get Historic Prices

Acckenn Calc **allows you to get the latest and historic prices** `(1 min avg. price)` of cryptoassets. Its use is not strictly required as part of the process of completing your transactions but provides a useful insight into the prices that Acckenn Calc will assign when it comes to value your cryptoassets in **Thai baht.**

**Data Sources:** The following price data sources are used.
- Bitkub
- Satang Pro
- Binance

# Acckenn NFTs

Acckenn Calc is an autonomously crypto tax calculator designed by tax accountants who have experience and understanding of crypto transactions.

## Organization Type

| Types  |   |
| ------ | - |
| Company Limited | At least 3 shareholders are required |
| Limited Partnership | At least 2 partners are required |

## Documents required

- Organization's name
- Location of head office and map
- Objective of the organization
- Registered capital
- Shareholders / Partners's details
  - Partnership: Name, address, age, occupation, telephone number, ID card and amount of capital of partners (at least 2 partners)
  - Company: Name, address, age, occupation, telephone number, ID card and number of shares holding of shareholders (at least 3 shareholders)
- Managing Director(s) / Partner(s)'s details
  - Partnership: Name, address, age, nationality, telephone number and ID card of managing partner(s)
  - Company: Name, address, age, nationality, telephone number and ID card of director(s)
